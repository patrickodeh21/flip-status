# Calendar Integration Guide

## How to Link Your Airbnb or VRBO Calendar

The **iCal / Calendar URL** field in your property settings allows you to sync your booking calendar from Airbnb, VRBO, or other platforms. This helps keep your availability up-to-date automatically.

---

## Where to Find the iCal URL

### **For Airbnb:**

1. **Log in to Airbnb** (use a desktop browser for easiest access)
2. Go to **Calendar** from your host dashboard
3. Click on the **listing** you want to sync
4. Look for **"Availability settings"** or **"Import/Export Calendar"**
5. Click **"Export Calendar"**
6. **Copy the iCal link** that appears (it will look like: `https://www.airbnb.com/calendar/ical/...`)

**Alternative Path:**
- Go to **Listings** → Select your property → **Calendar** → **Availability** → **Sync calendars** → **Export calendar**

---

### **For VRBO (Vacation Rentals by Owner):**

1. **Log in to VRBO** owner dashboard
2. Go to **Calendar** or **Property Settings**
3. Look for **"Calendar Import/Export"** or **"iCal"**
4. Click **"Export Calendar"**
5. **Copy the iCal URL** (it will look like: `https://www.vrbo.com/icalendar/...` or similar)

**Alternative Path:**
- **Dashboard** → **Calendar** → **Sync** → **Export** → Copy the iCal link

---

## How to Add the iCal URL to Your Property

### **When Creating a New Property:**
1. Go to **Properties** → **Add New Property**
2. Fill in the property details
3. Scroll to the **"iCal / Calendar URL (optional)"** field
4. **Paste the iCal link** from Airbnb or VRBO
5. Click **"Create Property"**

### **When Editing an Existing Property:**
1. Go to **Properties** → Click on the property you want to edit
2. Scroll to the **"iCal / Calendar URL (optional)"** field
3. **Paste the iCal link** from Airbnb or VRBO
4. Click **"Update"** to save

---

## Important Notes

✅ **The iCal URL is optional** - you can leave it blank if you don't use Airbnb or VRBO  
✅ **One URL per property** - each property should have its own unique iCal link  
✅ **Sync frequency** - The system will check for updates periodically (future feature)  
✅ **Multiple platforms** - If you list on both Airbnb and VRBO, you may need to choose which calendar to sync, or use a channel manager  

⚠️ **Security Note:** Keep your iCal URLs private - anyone with the link can view your booking calendar

---

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Where exactly is the iCal field?**  
A: It's located in the property form, right after the Latitude and Longitude fields.

**Q: Can I sync multiple calendars?**  
A: Currently, you can only add one iCal URL per property. If you need to sync multiple platforms, consider using a channel manager that provides a unified iCal feed.

**Q: The iCal URL isn't working**  
A: Make sure you copied the entire URL, including `https://`. The URL should be very long and contain random characters.

**Q: How often does it sync?**  
A: Calendar syncing is a planned feature. Currently, the field stores the URL for future implementation.
